Burger King Launches New 2 For $5 Mix n’ Match Deal Featuring The Big King Sandwich

February 8, 2022 Bob Miller Food News

Burger King is retiring their 2 for $6 Mix n’ Match offer and replacing it with a new 2 for $5 Mix n’ Match deal that features four sandwiches, including a couple of new additions: the Big King and Single Quarter Pound King.

Advertisment

Story continues below

As part of the revamped deal, you get any two of the following menu items for $5:

  • Big King Sandwich
  • Single Quarter Pound King Sandwich
  • Fish Sandwich
  • Original Chicken Sandwich

Burger King’s new 2 for $5 Mix n’ Match deal can be found at participating locations nationwide for a limited time starting February 10, 2022. Some locations are offering the promotion now in advance of the official release date.

It’s also worth noting that both the Big King and Single Quarter Pound King have been added to the fast-food chain’s 2 for $10 Mix n’ Match Meals.
